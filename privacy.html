Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Storage Demo Self Storage. We are committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our visitors, potential renters, and active customers. In this Privacy Policy, we provide detailed information on when and why we collect personal information, how we use it, the limited conditions under which we may disclose it to others, and how we keep it securely stored.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of personal data when you use our website or services:

  • Identity Data: First name, last name, date of birth, and identity verification documents for secure access.
  • Contact Data: Residential address, billing address, email address, and telephone/mobile numbers.
  • Financial Data: Secure payment card details and banking information processed via compliant payment gateways.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type/version, time zone settings, and operating system information regarding how you browse our digital assets.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to administer our business activities, provide the specific storage units you request, handle billing and collections, offer customer support, and communicate updates about our facilities or your rental agreement. We do not rent, sell, or trade personal data with third-party organizations for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies & Web Tracking

Our website utilizes standard technical cookies to keep track of your navigation session, remember your storage configuration preferences, and monitor broad traffic patterns. You can instruct your browser to refuse any or all cookies; however, disabling certain functional cookies may impact the ease of completing a digital reservation.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, seek corrections for any inaccuracies, request data deletion when it is no longer required for legitimate business purposes, or withdraw consent at any time. For all legal or personal data inquiries, please email our Data Protection Officer at privacy@StorageDemostorage.com.

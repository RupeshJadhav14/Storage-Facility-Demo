Terms & Conditions

Please read these terms carefully before accessing our facilities or booking a unit.

1. User Agreement

By signing a rental lease or accessing a unit managed by Storage Demo Self Storage, you agree to comply with all terms stated herein. The rental unit is specifically intended for the storage of dry personal goods, inventory, and legally owned vehicles. Prohibited items include hazardous materials, perishable foods, illicit goods, live animals, flammable chemicals, and any item emitting an odor.

2. Service Terms & Unit Access

Authorized renters are provided unique PIN access and keys allowing 24/7 entry. Passcodes must not be shared with unauthorized third parties. Storage Demo management reserves the right to enter your unit without prior notice in event of emergency (e.g., flood, fire) or pursuant to local law enforcement warrants.

3. Payments & Billing

All recurring rental charges are processed on the 1st of each calendar month via authorized payment methods. If payment is not received within 7 business days following the due date, gate access will be automatically suspended. Extended delinquencies exceeding 30 days may result in a formal lien placed upon the contents of the storage unit to cover unpaid balances.

4. Liability & Insurance

Storage Demo Self Storage does not act as a bailee or insurer of your property. All stored items are housed at the renter's sole risk. Renters are required to obtain individual storage insurance policies or provide active proof of existing homeowner's/business coverage extending to offsite inventory. Storage Demo assumes no direct liability for losses resulting from fire, theft, pests, or force majeure.

5. Cancellation & Moving Out

Renters operate on a flexible rolling monthly term. Notice of cancellation must be submitted in writing or via your digital account portal at least 14 days before the end of the current billing cycle. Upon termination, units must be completely vacated, cleaned, and free of all debris and locking mechanisms to avoid additional cleaning assessments.
